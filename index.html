Sweat

For our first clothe, an organic linen sweatshirt. Organic flax is grown in France without pesticides or chemical fertilizers, we are the leading producer, which makes it a more responsible option for the environment.To create this sweatshirt, we sourced the raw material directly from a local organic linen producer and bought the amount of linen needed for the production of several sweatshirts to avoid round trips. We then took charge of the creation and production in our workshop.

Buy Sweat

Pants

For our second clothe, we have created cotton and linen pants: organic cotton from Tanzania where irrigation is reasoned, this organic cotton does not pollute the water cycle.We then take care of the sewing and pleating.

Buy Pants

HandBag

For our latest creation, we chose to create a canvas handbag. The handbag is made from recycled canvas bags.To create this bag, we collected used canvas bags from terminals that we set up near so-called essential stores.We then brought them back to our workshop to assemble them and come up with the "perfect recycled bag".
